About Us

Zenix Ark is one of the only no-wipe clusters still on Ark Survival Evolved. It offers a unique, fast-paced experience with many mods and plugins that help balance and make the server a better place! Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer, Zenix Ark provides an engaging and dynamic environment where your progress is permanent.


Why Choose Zenix Ark?

  • No Wipe Policy: Build, tame, and conquer without the fear of losing everything to a server reset.
  • Balanced Gameplay: Carefully selected mods and plugins ensure a fair and enjoyable experience. Quality-of-life improvements & balanced PvP.
  • Custom Features: Enjoy a variety of custom mods, fresh spawns and custom recipes.
  • Events & Community: Participate in regular events keeping the gameplay fresh.
