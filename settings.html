Server Configuration

Everything you need to know about our rates, rules, and rulesets.

Official Rules

Mesh Cheating & Exploits

Using any exploit to get under the map, inside geometry, or access areas normally unplayable is strictly prohibited. This results in an immediate and permanent ban. Always build and fight transparently above ground.

Hacking & 3rd Party Software

Aimbots, ESP, memory editors, macros for rapid fire, or any other unauthorized 3rd party tools that give an unfair advantage are forbidden. We actively monitor all players and ban immediately.

Respect & Behavior

While this is a PvP server and trash talk is part of the game, strictly no slurs, racism, doxing, or real-life threats will be tolerated. Keep the battles ingame.

FOB & Raiding Rules

You may FOB to grief, but you must completely clean up your FOB structure once the raid/defense is concluded. Do not leave spam unnecessarily across the map.
